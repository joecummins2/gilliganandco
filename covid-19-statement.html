Covid 19 Statement

DUE TO THE CURRENT SITUATION REGARDING COVID-19 GILLIGAN & CO ARE OFFERING A RANGE OF APPOINTMENT TYPES DEPENDING ON WHAT THE CLIENT IS MOST COMFORTABLE WITH:

- We are offering free of charge home visits. The agent will be wearing a mask and can provide a mask for the Client.- We are offering office appointments. We will hold the meeting in a specious room where social distancing can be practiced. The agent will be wearing a mask and can provide a mask for the Client.- We are offering video appointments. We can arrange a video appointment with the Client using applications such as Zoom or FaceTime.- We are offering telephone appointments. We can arrange a telephone appointment with the Client. Gilligan & Co usually operate on a two appointment system. The first appointment is to discuss your situation and your requirements and to obtain as much information as possible to enable us to draft your documents. The second appointment is to explain the documents to you so that you can be sure that they meet your requirements. Gilligan & Co offer an Attestation Service that supervises the signing and witnessing of your Will(s). This is only possible where a home visit or office appointment has been arranged. If this is not possible then documents will be sent in the post and a video or telephone appointment will be arranged to explain your documents.
